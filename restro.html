About Bellaire Sushi Corner

Since 2009, Bellaire Sushi Corner has been proudly serving fresh, handcrafted sushi in the heart of Bellaire, Texas. Known for its warm hospitality and creative culinary approach, our restaurant offers a unique fusion of traditional Japanese flavors with global influences.

At Bellaire, sushi isn't just a dish—it's an experience. From the delicate simplicity of classic nigiri to bold, flavor-packed rolls, our menu features something for every palate. We specialize in innovative fusion rolls inspired by world cuisines—imagine sushi with a zesty Mexican twist, a fiery Indian kick, or the fragrant spices of Thai cooking. Whether you're a sushi purist or an adventurous foodie, you'll find a variety of options to satisfy your cravings.

Every dish is crafted with the finest ingredients, a deep respect for tradition, and a passion for flavor. Come enjoy a cozy, relaxed dining atmosphere by the lake and discover why Bellaire Sushi Corner has been a local favorite for over a decade.
